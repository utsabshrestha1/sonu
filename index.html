About Us

Hi Guys. Sonu's Mandala Ranch is an online based art company where we supply or sell our customers the art. You can choose any of my previously made art or order your own concept. You tell us your concept, I turn it into art. We share our handmade mandalas in very affordable prices, perfect for gifting your loved ones. You can also hang the art in you wall and make your wall look better in your room. Mandala is a symbol of dream. They bring about a feeling of relaxation. The sole purpose of mandala is to tranform ordinary minds into enlightened ones and assist in healing. Mandalas not only focus on your attention but also allow you to express your creative side.
